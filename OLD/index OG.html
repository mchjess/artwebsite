About

Jessica Lee is a practicing artist based in Australia. She has a Bachelor of Writing from the University of Canberra, a Diploma in Art Therapy, and has studied Fine Arts at NIDA, VCA and Monash University.

Her work as a therapist and educator nourishes her lust for understanding. What she learns she brings to her work, drawing ever deeper meaning and connections between concepts, emotional responses and the foundational impulses which drive creative expression at all levels of practice.
